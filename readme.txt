Booking Calendar and Notification is a plug-in designed for easy booking to your business. Set and display your date(s) whether it is avaiable, unavailable or booked.

Manage bookings from your customer with statistical graphs.

== Core Plugin Features ==

* Booking Form - using shortcode
* Set/Manage Calendar Availability, Unavailability and Booked
* Multiple Calendars
* Manage Bookings
* Customer List
* Booking Settings
* Calendar Settings
* Email Settings & Notification
* Generate Report
* Statistic Graphs & Summary

== Installation ==

1. Click Install button and Activate or Upload `booking-calenar-and-notification.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

= Configuration =

1. Create page and insert [schortcode] of your calendar.
2. Set your calendar availability.
3. Configure settings for your calendar and email notification.
4. Enjoy Managing of bookings form your customers.
